Our Services

1. Facial Threading

Facial threading is an ancient hair removal technique that uses cotton threads to remove unwanted facial hair. It provides precise control, allowing our estheticians to shape your eyebrows and remove fine facial hair for a clean and defined look.

2. Eyebrow Shaping

Our eyebrow shaping service focuses on creating the perfect eyebrow arch and shape to enhance your facial features. Our skilled estheticians work with you to achieve the desired eyebrow shape that complements your face shape and personal style.

3. Upper Lip Threading

Upper lip threading is a quick and effective way to remove unwanted hair from the upper lip area. Threading provides precise hair removal, leaving your skin smooth and ensuring a clean upper lip appearance.

4. Full Face Threading

Our full face threading service covers all areas of the face, including the forehead, cheeks, chin, and jawline. This comprehensive service provides thorough facial hair removal, leaving your skin looking smooth and radiant.

5. Eyebrow Tinting

Enhance the color and definition of your eyebrows with our eyebrow tinting service. Our estheticians use high-quality tint to match your eyebrow color to your desired shade, giving your brows a more defined and polished appearance.

6. Eyebrow Waxing

Achieve a clean and well-defined look with our eyebrow waxing service. Our skilled estheticians use gentle waxing techniques to remove unwanted hair and shape your eyebrows, providing a smooth and long-lasting result.
